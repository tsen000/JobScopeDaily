## 🚀 JobScopeDaily
**Your daily compass for career growth and insightful news analysis.**

JobScopeDaily is a dedicated repository archiving our daily newsletter issues. We bridge the gap between job seekers and the evolving landscape of the Bangladeshi job market by providing curated updates, corporate insights, and sharp editorial perspectives.

---

## 📌 Key Features
Each issue in this repository is structured to provide maximum value:

- **Editorial & Analysis:** Deep dives into national administrative reforms, economic shifts and so on.

- **Company Spotlights:** In-depth profiles of Bangladesh’s leading conglomerates, individual company analysis and also Job Seeker Insights.

- **Curated Job Updates:** Government Job Sectors, Private Job Sectors and also IT & Specialized Job Sectors

- **National News Digest:** A summary of the day’s most critical news collected from online newspaper.
