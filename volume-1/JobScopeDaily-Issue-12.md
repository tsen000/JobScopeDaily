Most Asked Government Job Interview Questions

1. Tell us about yourself
-Thank you, Sir/Madam. I am a graduate in Business Administration. I have a strong interest in public service and administrative work. I believe my academic background and disciplined mindset make me suitable for this position.

2. Why do you want a government job?
-I want a government job because it allows me to serve the public with responsibility and integrity. It also offers a stable work environment where I can contribute in the long term.

3. Why should we select you?
-I am sincere, disciplined, and willing to learn. I follow rules, respect authority, and I am committed to performing my duties responsibly.

4. What do you know about our department?
-Your department works to implement government policies at the field level and ensure public service delivery. I understand that transparency and accountability are key responsibilities here.

5. What are your strengths?
-My strengths include honesty, time management, and adaptability. I remain calm under pressure and try to complete tasks accurately.”

6. What is your weakness?
-I sometimes take extra time to ensure accuracy, but I am learning to balance speed and precision.”
