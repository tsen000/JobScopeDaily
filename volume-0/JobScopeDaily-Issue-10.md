Although there is no new job circular update today, this is an important time to focus on preparation for upcoming Bank General Officer (GO) recruitment exams.

Bank General Officer (GO) Role কী?
-Bank General Officer মূলত customer service, account management, loan processing, banking compliance এবং দৈনন্দিন administrative কাজ পরিচালনা করেন। এই পদে accuracy, analytical thinking এবং professional communication skill খুবই গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।

Bank GO Exam Structure (সাধারণত)
-Preliminary Examination (MCQ), Written Examination, Viva / Interview. এই structure অনুযায়ী প্রস্তুতি নিলে success rate অনেক বেড়ে যায়।

Subjects (যেগুলোতে বেশি গুরুত্ব দিতে হবে)
-বাংলা: ব্যাকরণ, বাক্য সংশোধন, শব্দার্থ
-English: Grammar, Vocabulary, Reading Comprehension
-গণিত: Arithmetic, Percentage, Ratio, Problem Solving
-General Knowledge: Banking, Economy, Bangladesh & International Affairs

Banking specific
-Basic banking terms (Interest Rate, CRR, SLR, Liquidity), Bangladesh Bank-এর circular ও financial news, Time-bound MCQ practice, Previous Bank GO question.
