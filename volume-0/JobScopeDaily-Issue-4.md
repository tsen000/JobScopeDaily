Difference between Govt job and Private job:
A government job offers high job security, fixed salary scales, structured promotions, retirement benefits, regular allowances, fixed working hours, lower work pressure, strong social recognition, and transparent exam-based recruitment,
Whereas a private job depends on company performance, provides flexible and performance-based salaries, faster but less structured career growth, limited retirement benefits, variable allowances, longer working hours, higher work pressure, role-based recognition, and skill- or interview-based recruitment.

Key skills for Government jobs:
Include strong general knowledge, analytical and writing ability, exam-oriented preparation, understanding of rules and procedures, discipline, integrity, communication skills, and long-term commitment to public service.

Key skills for Private jobs:
Include technical and job-specific expertise, problem-solving ability, adaptability, communication and teamwork skills, time management, performance orientation, digital literacy, and the ability to work in fast-paced, target-driven environments.
